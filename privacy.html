3. Cookies Policy

HARSHIT DESIGNERS uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and deliver relevant content. Cookies are small data files created by a web server and stored on your device that help us track usage patterns and recognize return visitors.

We use the following tracking technologies:

  • Cookies: Help track online preferences, identify return visitors, and customize your web experience so you don't need to re-enter information on every visit

  • Log Files: Used to track and monitor member usage patterns across our website

Third-party advertising companies may also place cookies on your browser when ads are displayed. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some website features may not function correctly as a result.
